Warnings and information

Good news! Almost any available gaming device today can be given custom firmware or some form of hack to run homebrew apps and games on. Even the most obscure consoles have decicated communities reverse engineering their favourite devices to improve the end-user experience.

However, not all consoles share the same kind of architecture. Some devices require much more work or knowledge to unlock, and thus I have arranged the different difficulties of each process. Whilst learning to hack your consoles can be rewarding, it can also ocasionally be a dangerous process IF you aren't following the guides. Many beginners make the mistake of using youtube videos to learn the process, which can often become outdated fast. The safest way to go about hacking your console is using written text guides maintained by knowledgeable community members. Each of the best resources are laid out below.

With that out of the way, don't let the warnings deter you. Learning or even simply looking into these steps can be very beneficial in gaining tech literacy, or setting you onto the path to learn new skills. Although I'm sure, like me, you just wanna play some games too so let's get on with it!
